Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Overview

PeerLink is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect and store as little data as possible — in fact, virtually none.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Messages, files, or media you send or receive
  • Your identity, email, or personal information
  • Message history or conversation logs
  • IP addresses (beyond what STUN servers temporarily use for NAT traversal)

3. How Your Data Flows

All messages and files travel directly between browsers using WebRTC DataChannel. STUN servers (operated by Google) are used only to discover your public IP for connection establishment — they do not relay or log your data.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

PeerLink does not use cookies, localStorage, or any persistent browser storage. Session state exists only in memory and is cleared when you close the tab.

5. Third-Party Services

We use public STUN servers (stun.l.google.com) for NAT traversal. These servers see your public IP address momentarily during connection establishment, subject to Google's privacy policy.

We use Google Fonts and Tailwind CSS CDN for styling. These services may log basic request metadata per their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

PeerLink is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

For privacy concerns, please reach out via the project repository.
